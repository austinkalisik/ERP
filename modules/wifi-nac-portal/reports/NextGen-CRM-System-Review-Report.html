NextGen CRM System Review Report

Feature coverage, implementation details, screenshots, and recommended next review items.

Prepared for: Senior management / senior technical review

Prepared on: 18 May 2026

System: NextGen CRM, Laravel 13, React 19, Inertia, MySQL, Tailwind CSS/shadcn UI

Local review URL: http://127.0.0.1:8001/login

Executive Summary

The CRM is currently built as a Laravel and React business system for managing Nextgen Technology Limited PNG customer records, service requests, subscriptions, domain hosting, renewals, payments, invoice files, credits, users, roles, and customer self-service access.

The system already covers the main operational goals requested for client listing, subscriptions by service category, renewal monitoring, invoice/payment attachments, and service outage credits that extend expiry dates.

Requirement Coverage

RequirementStatusImplemented Details
List all client informationAvailableCustomer register stores company name, contact person, email, phone, industry, status, website, address, notes, follow-up date, and related request counts.
List subscriptions for each client by serviceAvailableSubscription register links each subscription to a client and categorizes services as Domain Hosting, Internet Service, GPS, Email Hosting, Website Hosting, or Other.
Monitor expiry dates and renewalsAvailableSubscriptions store start date, expiry date, renewal cycle, and status. The Renewals page searches renewal records by month and updates selected renewal dates.
Attach payment and invoice filesAvailablePayment records support PDF, image, Word, and Excel attachments up to 10 MB, including invoice number, payment reference, payment date, covered period, amount, and notes.
Track payments and unused creditsAvailableCredits record outage start/end dates, month count, value, reason, and automatically extend the subscription expiry date. Example: a March-April 2026 ISP outage creates a two-month credit beyond the original expiry.
Automatic reminder notificationsNeeds decisionRenewal monitoring exists through the Renewals page. Automated email/SMS reminders are not confirmed as implemented and should be reviewed as a next enhancement.

Features Added

Customer Management

  • Customer dashboard and customer listing.
  • Create, update, and admin delete customer records.
  • Customer status tracking for lead, active, inactive, and suspended clients.
  • Follow-up date and notes for account management.

Subscriptions

  • Client-linked subscription register.
  • Service categories for domain hosting, internet service, GPS, email hosting, website hosting, and other services.
  • Start date, expiry date, renewal cycle, status, amount, reference, and notes.
  • Search and service summary counts.

Payments and Invoices

  • Payment records per subscription.
  • Payment period start and end dates.
  • Invoice number, payment reference, amount, and notes.
  • Invoice/payment file upload and secure download by staff users.

Credits

  • Service outage credit records.
  • Credit start/end dates and calculated credit months.
  • Optional credit value and required reason.
  • Automatic subscription expiry extension when a credit is applied.

Additional Modules

  • Renewals: monthly renewal search covering subscription expiry and domain/hosting renewal records.
  • Service Requests: support request tracking with customer, assignee, status, priority, service type, quote, renewal, and internal notes.
  • Domain Registrations: domain registration workflow and domain listing/reporting.
  • User and Role Management: admin, staff, and customer roles with customer-linked portal access.
  • Customer Portal: customer users can view their own service requests and submit requests.
  • Bulk Email Validator: validates email lists and supports sending bulk email when SMTP credentials are configured.
  • Admin Settings: branding, logo, SMTP, and system settings support.
  • Security: Laravel Fortify authentication, email verification middleware, role checks, two-factor support, and restricted staff/admin actions.
